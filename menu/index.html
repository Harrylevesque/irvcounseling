Welcome to Irvington Counseling Center

We are dedicated to providing compassionate, accessible, and effective mental health care for individuals and families in our community. Learn more about our mission and the people who make it possible.

About Us

The Irvington Counseling Center offers therapeutic clinical & psychiatric services to the community. Our staff are professionally trained in specialty areas of counseling to address various mental health needs in our community. There is collaboration among local agencies, schools, hospitals, courts, & the township of Irvington to promote a healthy and safe space to talk about mental health.
(Read about our mission)

We strive to help our patients lead a happier life and remind them that they are not alone in their journey to mental wellness. Here at ICC, our goal is to ensure that each patient receives optimal care regardless of their circumstances.
(Read about Mental Health and Therapy)

Psychiatric Services: We offer psychiatric evaluations and medication monitoring. Our APNs are equipped to give you further treatment if counseling does not help to alleviate your symptoms.
